
Welcome Guide
Thank you for choosing Town Square Fort Mill. We are honored to provide an interactive, meaningful, and thoughtful experience for your loved one. This guide is designed to give you an overview of our center, the services we provide, and the policies and procedures that help us create a safe and engaging environment.
Center Information
Town Square Fort Mill is open Monday through Friday from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. We observe several holiday closures, including:
New Year’s Day
Memorial Day
Independence Day (July 4th)
Labor Day
Thanksgiving Day, with early closure the day before Thanksgiving
Christmas Day, with early closure on Christmas Eve
New Year’s Eve (early closure)
What’s Included Each Day
Each day at Town Square includes a continental breakfast, a hot lunch, and an afternoon snack. In addition, our team provides medication management, toileting and hygiene support, and nursing oversight by licensed professionals. Members participate in enriching, structured programs tailored to their cognitive and physical needs, all facilitated by compassionate and certified staff.
Billing and Payments
Billing is processed on the first of each month, or the next business day if the first falls on a weekend or holiday. Families receive an invoice for the upcoming month’s membership package as well as any additional services requested. Payment is due upon invoicing.
Cancellations and Make-Up Policy
If a member is unable to attend a scheduled day, we ask that you notify the center at least forty-eight hours in advance whenever possible. If less notice is provided, the day will still be charged, but make-up days may be scheduled. These must be used within thirty calendar days on a non-regularly scheduled day.
Drop-In Policy
Drop-in days are only available to current members, as all attendees must be fully registered and have completed physician’s forms. Families interested in arranging a drop-in day should contact the Center Director or Coordinator at least one business day in advance.
Transportation Services
Transportation is available from prearranged locations for $10 each way, or door-to-door pick-up for $20 each way. Town Square also works with contracted vendors to help families arrange additional transportation solutions as needed.
Extra Services
Members may access additional services for an extra fee. Shower assistance is available for $25 per shower, with families asked to provide clothing and personal care items in advance. Laundry services are available for $5 per load. Barber and salon services may be arranged directly by contacting our stylist at 317-617-3021. For late pick-ups, families are charged $25 for the first fifteen minutes, and $2 for each additional minute thereafter, not to exceed thirty minutes.
Additional Care Options
Town Square partners with licensed third-party providers to make additional services available to families. These may include primary care, psychiatric care, and physical, occupational, or speech therapy. These services are not included in membership and must be arranged directly with providers, though our staff will gladly assist in coordination.
Communication
We are committed to open communication with families. Each month, members receive a calendar of activities and a lunch menu. Other notices or reminders may be sent home or electronically. Communication specific to an individual member is handled directly with their primary contact by the Center Director or Nurse. Families are encouraged to review reminders to help members fully participate in themed events and celebrations. For questions or updates, please contact us at 803-591-9898 or mpiscatelli@townsquare.net.
Inclement Weather and Emergencies
The safety of our members and staff is always our highest priority. Transportation providers make their own service decisions during inclement weather and will notify families directly of changes. If the center closes due to weather or another emergency, families will be notified by email, and closure information may also be posted on our Facebook page. If an early closure becomes necessary, families will be contacted directly by a staff member. In the event of an evacuation, Town Square will coordinate an alternate location and communicate it immediately to responsible parties.
At Town Square Fort Mill, our commitment is to provide excellent care, engaging programs, and peace of mind to families. We look forward to welcoming your loved one into our Town Square family.
