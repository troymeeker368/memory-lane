
 
We are looking forward to seeing you at Town Square! 
Town Square Fort Mill is a licensed Adult Day Center within South Carolina. To remain compliant with state regulations and to better know our new member, complete the following paperwork and return to the Center Director or Enrollment Manager. 
Welcome Guide
Face Sheet and Biography 
Membership Agreement & Exhibit A 
Notice of Privacy Practices 
Member Statement of Rights 
Photo Consent 
Ancillary Charges Notice
Insurance and/or POA paperwork upload
Items needed prior to first day at the center: 
Copy of insurance cards (can be uploaded via DocuSign)
Copy of POA (if applicable, can be uploaded via DocuSign)
Items needed on first day at the center:
Change of clothes 
Medications in labeled prescription bottles (if applicable) 
Personal care products (if applicable) 
If you have any questions or concerns about completing the forms, please do not hesitate to contact the center and a staff member will be available to assist you.
